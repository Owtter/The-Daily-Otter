What's The Daily Otter?

We at The Daily Otter aim to provide you with the most interesting and up to date otter content. Be it stories and images of our furry celebrities, breaking news in the world of otters or newly discovered facts.

Who's behind The Daily Otter?

We are a group of otter enthusiast with the goal of spreading awareness of their diminishing population due to pollution, deforestation and overfishing. Also, we think they're cute.
